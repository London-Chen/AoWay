How to Play

Getting started with Monster Survivors is simple but mastering it is the real challenge. Use your mouse or touch controls to move your character around the battlefield while automatically attacking nearby enemies. Collect experience points and power-ups dropped by defeated monsters to upgrade your abilities and unlock new weapons.

The key to survival lies in positioning and timing. Stay mobile to avoid being overwhelmed, but don't forget to collect the valuable resources scattered across the battlefield.
